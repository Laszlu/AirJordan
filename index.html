This Website is dedicated to be an archive of the first iterations of Michael Jordans signature sneaker. There is a page about the history and the OG colorways for every Model

The Jordan brand was created in 1985 as a collaboration between Nike and Michael Jordan. Initially Jordan wanted to sign with Converse, but his Parents made him take a meeting with Nike, where he was offered to create a whole brand around him. The product lineup started with the Nike Air Jordan 1, which was designed by Nikes creative director, Peter Moore. The next model, the Nike Air Jordan 2 was crafted in Italy using premium materials. Beginning with the Nike Air Jordan 3, designer Tinker Hatfield the Air Jordan line. He also created the revolutionary Nike Air Max 1. Hatfield was the lead designer for Jordan Brand until the Nike Air Jordan 15.
